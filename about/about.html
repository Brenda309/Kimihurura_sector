Kimihurura Sector

Leafy and hilly, Kimihurura is home to international and African dining on outdoor terraces
with panoramas across the city and surrounding hills. The Kigali Heights complex includes
a mall selling casual fashions, while the Campaign Against Genocide Museum relates the tragic
events of 1994. A few upscale hotels cater to delegates at the Kigali Convention Centre,
featuring a landmark dome that is lit up at night.
